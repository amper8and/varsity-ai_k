## 1. Executive Summary

### 1.1 Vision
To create a comprehensive, AI-powered community platform that unlocks the lifelong value of UNIZIK's student and alumni affinity. The platform serves as a digital ecosystem where the university community discovers exclusive deals, accesses AI-powered services, and maintains lifelong connections with their alma mater.

### 1.2 Opportunity
Nnamdi Azikiwe University represents a significant untapped market:

| Community Segment | Estimated Size | Monthly Spend (₦) |
|-------------------|----------------|-------------------|
| Students | ~35,000 | ₦45,000 - ₦85,000 |
| Alumni | ~150,000+ | ₦150,000 - ₦400,000 |
| Friends & Family | ~250,000+ | ₦80,000 - ₦200,000 |
| **Total Addressable Community** | **~435,000+** | **Significant Aggregated Spend** |

### 1.3 Value Proposition
- **For Students:** Exclusive discounts, AI academic assistant, campus services, career opportunities
- **For Alumni:** Lifelong benefits, networking, continued education access, exclusive partner deals
- **For University:** New revenue stream, enhanced community engagement, data-driven insights
- **For Brand Partners:** Direct access to engaged, verified university community segments
- **For MTN:** Increased subscriber loyalty, data usage, service adoption

## 2. Product Overview

### 2.1 Product Name
**"UNIZIK Connect"** (Working Title) - *Subject to stakeholder approval*

Alternative names considered:
- ZIK Hive
- UNIZIK Plus
- ZIK Community

### 2.2 Platform Definition
UNIZIK Connect is a mobile-first AI-powered community platform that combines:
1. **AI Academic Assistant** - Personalized tutoring and campus support
2. **Deals & Rewards Marketplace** - Exclusive partner offers and promotions
3. **Community Services** - Accommodation, transportation, career services
4. **Campus Hub** - University news, events, and administrative services
5. **Alumni Network** - Lifelong connection and exclusive benefits

### 2.3 Key Differentiators
- **Contextual AI** powered by university-specific knowledge
- **Lifelong community** model (not just current students)
- **Integrated mobility** services via MTN
- **Revenue-generating** for the university
- **Seamless, modern** mobile experience

## 3. Target Users & Personas

### 3.1 Primary Personas

#### Persona 1: "Chioma" - The Freshman Student
- **Age:** 17-19
- **Status:** First-year undergraduate
- **Needs:** Academic support, accommodation, budget-friendly options, campus navigation
- **Tech Comfort:** High
- **Primary Goals:** Succeed academically, save money, make connections
- **Pain Points:** Overwhelmed by campus life, limited budget, needs reliable information

#### Persona 2: "Emeka" - The Final Year Student
- **Age:** 23-25
- **Status:** Final year student
- **Needs:** Career preparation, job opportunities, internship placements, affordable living
- **Tech Comfort:** Very High
- **Primary Goals:** Secure employment, maintain grades, transition to alumni status
- **Pain Points:** Job market competition, financial pressure, career uncertainty

#### Persona 3: "Dr. Ngozi" - The Young Alumnus
- **Age:** 28-35
- **Status:** 5-10 years post-graduation
- **Needs:** Professional networking, continued learning, lifestyle benefits, giving back
- **Tech Comfort:** High
- **Primary Goals:** Career advancement, maintain university connection, access exclusive perks
- **Pain Points:** Limited alumni engagement channels, wants value for loyalty

#### Persona 4: "Chief Obi" - The Established Alumnus
- **Age:** 45-60
- **Status:** Senior professional/business owner
- **Needs:** High-value partnerships, investment opportunities, mentorship platform, prestige benefits
- **Tech Comfort:** Moderate
- **Primary Goals:** Give back to alma mater, access premium services, expand network
- **Pain Points:** Complex digital interfaces, wants recognition for status

### 3.2 Secondary Personas
- Parents of students (financial services, monitoring tools)
- Prospective students (admissions information, campus preview)
- University staff (administrative tools, communication)

## 5. Technical Architecture

### 5.1 System Overview
Based on the AI-Mobile architecture:

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                    EXPERIENCE LAYER                        │
│         (Mobile App, Web Portal, Dashboards)               │
└───────────────────────┬─────────────────────────────────────┘
                        │
┌───────────────────────▼─────────────────────────────────────┐
│                     AI AGENT LAYER                         │
│    (Academic Assistant, Support Bot, Recommendation        │
│                    Engine, Tutor)                          │
└───────────────────────┬─────────────────────────────────────┘
                        │
┌───────────────────────▼─────────────────────────────────────┐
│                  COMPUTATION LAYER                         │
│   (LLM Models, ML Algorithms, Data Processing,             │
│              Governance Engine)                            │
└───────────────────────┬─────────────────────────────────────┘
                        │
┌───────────────────────▼─────────────────────────────────────┐
│                  CONNECTION LAYER                          │
│  (MTN APIs, University Systems, Partner APIs, Payment      │
│              Gateways, MCP Protocols)                      │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### 5.2 Platform Requirements

#### Mobile App
- **iOS:** Version 14+
- **Android:** Version 8.0+
- **Offline Support:** Core features accessible offline
- **Data Optimization:** Efficient for 2G/3G networks

#### Backend
- Cloud-native architecture
- API-first design
- Microservices for scalability
- Real-time data synchronization

#### Security
- End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
- NIST AI Risk Management Framework compliance
- ISO 27001 security standards
- Regular third-party security audits
- Data privacy compliance (NDPR)

### 5.3 AI/ML Components
- **LLM Integration:** Context-aware responses with university-specific training
- **Recommendation Engine:** Personalized deals and content
- **Natural Language Processing:** Multi-language support
- **Fraud Detection:** Transaction monitoring

## 6. User Experience Flows

### 6.1 Onboarding Flow
1. **Download** app from store
2. **Welcome** screen with value proposition
3. **Phone number** verification (MTN integration)
4. **Identity verification** (Student ID/Alumni record)
5. **Profile setup** (interests, preferences)
6. **Tutorial** highlighting key features
7. **First deal** unlock (welcome offer)

### 6.2 Daily Use Flows

#### Student - "Find a Deal"
1. Open app → Home dashboard
2. Browse categories or search
3. View deal details
4. Save or redeem immediately
5. Present QR code at merchant / Use online
6. Rate and review experience

#### Student - "Get Academic Help"
1. Tap "ZIK AI" assistant
2. Ask question or upload problem
3. Receive guided explanation
4. Follow-up questions
5. Save to study notes

#### Alumni - "Network & Benefits"
1. Access alumni section
2. Browse job opportunities
3. Connect with mentees
4. Access exclusive partner deals
5. RSVP to alumni events

## 7. Business Model & Revenue

### 7.1 Revenue Streams

| Source | Description | Split |
|--------|-------------|-------|
| Partner Commission | Fee on transactions through platform | Partners → Platform |
| Listing Fees | Premium placement for partners | Partners → Platform |
| Platform Fees | Subscription for AI services | Users → Platform |
| MTN Channel Fees | Revenue share on MTN services | MTN → Platform |
| Advertising | Targeted promotions | Advertisers → Platform |

### 7.2 Revenue Distribution
Based on the UNISA model adapted for Nigeria:

```
Total Platform Revenue
        │
        ├── MTN EBU Share (25-30%)
        │
        └── Net Platform Revenue
                │
                ├── UNIZIK University Share (40%)
                │
                └── AIM Share (60%)
```

## 10. Risk Assessment & Mitigation

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
